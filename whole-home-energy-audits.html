COMMON QUESTIONS

Whole Home Energy Audit FAQs

What happens during a whole home energy audit?↓

We perform a comprehensive inspection including blower door testing, infrared thermal imaging, duct leakage testing, insulation inspection, and a detailed report with prioritized recommendations and cost estimates.

How much does an energy audit cost in Calhoun GA?↓

Our standard whole home energy audit is $299–$499 depending on home size. The cost is often offset by utility rebates and the savings from implementing the recommendations.

Will an energy audit find problems I can fix myself?↓

Yes. Many issues like air leaks around windows, doors, and outlets, insufficient attic insulation, and duct leaks can be addressed affordably. We provide a clear prioritized list.

Do you help with rebates and incentives?↓

Yes. We guide you through Georgia Power, federal tax credits, and local incentives available for insulation, HVAC, windows, and other efficiency upgrades.

How long does a whole home energy audit take?↓

Most audits take 2–4 hours depending on home size and complexity. You receive a detailed written report within 3–5 business days.
