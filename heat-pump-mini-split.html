Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to install a heat pump in Georgia?
Heat pump installation costs in Calhoun and NW Georgia typically range based on system size, brand, and whether ductwork modifications are needed. We provide free in-home estimates and can often find rebates that offset costs. Call (706) 629-0749.
Are heat pumps worth it in Georgia winters?
Yes — modern heat pumps work efficiently even in Georgia's climate. Combined with proper insulation and air sealing (our whole-home approach), they deliver excellent comfort and lower energy bills year-round.
What is the difference between a heat pump and a mini split?
A central heat pump uses existing ductwork. A mini split (ductless) system installs indoor units in specific rooms with no ducts required — ideal for additions, older homes, or zoning control.
Do you offer rebates or incentives for heat pump installation?
We help customers take advantage of current federal, state, and utility rebates for high-efficiency heat pumps. Our BPI certification ensures your project qualifies for maximum incentives.
Can a mini split replace my existing furnace?
In many cases yes, especially with good insulation. We evaluate your whole home's energy performance first — that's the Anderson difference. We don't just swap equipment; we fix the house.
