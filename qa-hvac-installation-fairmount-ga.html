How do I know if I need hvac installation in my Fairmount home?

Look for these flags: a system 12-15+ years old that's needing major repairs (compressor, coil, heat exchanger), bills climbing year over year, rooms that won't condition properly, or comfort that's been off since the original install. Rural properties with propane heat and older AC equipment are good candidates to consider dual-fuel heat pump replacements that reduce propane burn in shoulder seasons. Our BPI-certified assessment looks at the actual numbers — load, ductwork, envelope — to decide whether replacement makes sense or repairs and improvements buy you more time.

Is hvac installation covered under warranty in Fairmount?
