# Website Audit: Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation

**Date:** 2026-05-13
**Audited URLs:** 
- Homepage: `https://shylohchavez.github.io/anderson-hai/`
- About Page: `https://shylohchavez.github.io/anderson-hai/about.html`

As requested, here is a brutally honest, actionable audit of the Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation website, graded by an SEO and digital marketing expert.

---

### 1. Traditional SEO (Rating: 6/10)
**Feedback:** The core keywords are present (HVAC, insulation, weatherization, Calhoun GA), and the basic heading structure (H1, H2, H3) is utilized well. The page titles are decent (e.g., "About Anderson Heating... | Formerly John Anderson Service Co. Since 1978").
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- **Schema Markup:** Needs LocalBusiness schema and FAQ schema to explicitly tell Google about the 519 Pine Street address, the 1978 founding date, and the (706) 629-0749 phone number.
- **Meta Descriptions:** Ensure unique, click-enticing meta descriptions exist for every page, highlighting the 632+ 4.8★ reviews.
- **Content Depth:** The homepage service blurbs are a bit thin. They need more supporting localized text.

### 2. GSEO / AI SEO (Rating: 7/10)
**Feedback:** AI engines look for authoritative, clearly structured entities. The site does a good job defining *who* you are (John Anderson, Shyloh, 1978, BPI Certified, 519 Pine Street). The phrase "Most companies just swap the box. We fix the whole house." is highly quotable for an LLM summarization.
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Create a dedicated "FAQ" section formatted cleanly with direct questions and answers (e.g., "Why is my power bill so high after a new AC install?"). AI search engines love consuming and citing direct Q&A formats.

### 3. Content Quality (Rating: 8/10)
**Feedback:** The messaging is the strongest part of the site. The narrative of "HVAC is King. Everything Else Supports It." and the origin story on the About page make the company feel authentic and highly competent.
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Make sure to feature real photos of the in-house laser cutters and custom sheet metal shop. Telling the story is great; showing it proves it.

### 4. Trust Signals (Rating: 9/10)
**Feedback:** Excellent. You've front-loaded the 632+ Google Reviews, the 4.8-star rating, 48 years in business, and BPI/NATE/ACCA certifications. Mentioning local awards (Clayton County, North Georgia Community Action) builds massive local trust.
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Embed a live Google Reviews widget rather than just stating the number, so users can read the latest reviews without leaving the site.

### 5. Conversion Optimization (Rating: 5/10)
**Feedback:** The phone number (706) 629-0749 is present, but it needs to be an omnipresent, sticky header. The CTAs on the homepage ("Learn more →") are passive. 
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Change passive CTAs to action-oriented ones: "Book a Free Estimate", "Schedule Your Energy Audit", "Call Now".
- Integrate your HouseCall Pro booking link directly so people can schedule online 24/7 without calling.

### 6. Mobile Responsiveness (Rating: TBD / Assume 6/10)
**Feedback:** While the underlying HTML structure is clean, standard GitHub Pages deployments often lack mobile-optimized touch targets.
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Ensure the phone number is a clickable `tel:` link on all instances (it is on the About page, make sure it is in the header/footer of the Homepage). 
- Ensure buttons have at least a 44x44px touch area for fat thumbs.

### 7. Brand Consistency (Rating: 8/10)
**Feedback:** The transition from "John Anderson Service Company" to "Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation" is handled beautifully. "The Paws-itive Choice" and Gypsy the dog provide a friendly, memorable mascot.
**Actionable Fixes:** 
- Ensure the specific brand colors (Purple/Orange) are strictly adhered to in the CSS. Don't rely on generic hex codes; use the exact brand guidelines.

### 8. What's MISSING that should be there?
- **Online Booking Widget:** As a modern HVAC company using HouseCall Pro, you absolutely need a "Book Online" widget.
- **Financing Details:** You mention Optimus/GreenSky/Wisetack briefly. There should be a dedicated page explaining how homeowners can finance a $15k system for $150/month.
- **Team Photos:** The About page mentions John, Shyloh, and Gypsy. Where are their pictures? People buy from people.

### 9. What's WRONG that needs fixing?
- **Passive "Read More" loops:** Don't just link to sub-pages without giving the user a chance to convert immediately on the homepage.
- **Lack of Emergency Framing:** "24/7 Emergency Service" is buried in a paragraph. If someone's AC dies in August, they need to see "24/7 EMERGENCY REPAIR - CALL NOW" at the very top.

### 10. How to Rank #1 in Calhoun GA for HVAC
- **Hyper-Local Service Pages:** Create dedicated pages for "AC Repair Calhoun GA", "Heat Pump Installation Rome GA", etc.
- **GBP Synchronization:** Ensure the Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) on the website matches the Google Business Profile *exactly*.
- **Backlink Campaign:** Sponsor more local events (like the Boogie & Blues Festival mentioned) and ensure they link back to `anderson-hai.com` (or the live domain) from their websites.
- **Case Studies/Project Gallery:** Add a CompanyCam integration or project gallery showing before/after photos of ductwork and insulation jobs in Calhoun.

---
**Summary for the SEO Agent:**
The site's messaging and trust signals are top-tier. To take it to the next level, implement technical Schema markup, switch to aggressive conversion-focused CTAs (Book Online / HouseCall Pro), and build out hyper-local landing pages for specific cities and services.
