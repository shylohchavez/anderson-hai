COMMON QUESTIONS

Insulation & Air Sealing FAQs

How much can insulation save on my energy bill in Calhoun GA?↓

Proper insulation and air sealing often reduce heating and cooling costs by 20-40%. Many of our customers see payback in 3-5 years through lower utility bills.

What is BPI certification and why does it matter?↓

BPI (Building Performance Institute) certification means we use scientific testing — blower door tests, infrared cameras, and pressure diagnostics — instead of guessing. We measure before and after.

Do you do crawlspace encapsulation?↓

Yes. We specialize in sealed, conditioned crawlspaces with vapor barriers, insulation, and dehumidification. This stops moisture, mold, and makes your home healthier and more efficient.

How long does insulation installation take?↓

Most attic insulation jobs are completed in 1 day. Whole-home air sealing and wall insulation typically take 2-4 days depending on the size and condition of the home.

Will insulation make my new HVAC system work better?↓

Absolutely. Your HVAC is only as good as your home's envelope. We often recommend insulation and air sealing before or alongside new equipment so you get the full efficiency benefit.
