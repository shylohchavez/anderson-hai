COMMON QUESTIONS

Crawlspace Encapsulation FAQs

What is crawlspace encapsulation?↓

Crawlspace encapsulation seals your crawlspace with a heavy-duty vapor barrier on the floors and walls, combined with insulation and often a dehumidifier, turning it into a conditioned, dry space instead of a damp, moldy one.

How does crawlspace encapsulation help my home?↓

It stops moisture and mold, improves indoor air quality, reduces energy bills by making your HVAC more efficient, and can even increase your home's value and structural longevity.

Is crawlspace encapsulation worth the cost in Georgia?↓

Yes. Georgia's humid climate makes encapsulation one of the highest-ROI home improvements. Many customers see payback through lower energy bills and avoided mold repairs within 5-7 years.

How long does crawlspace encapsulation take?↓

Most residential crawlspaces are completed in 2-4 days depending on size, access, and whether insulation or dehumidification is added.

Do you offer financing for crawlspace work?↓

Yes. We offer financing through Optimus (GreenSky, Wisetack) and senior/military discounts to make this important upgrade affordable.
