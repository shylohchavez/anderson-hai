COMMON QUESTIONS

Duct Cleaning & Sealing FAQs

How often should I have my ducts cleaned in Calhoun GA?↓

We recommend professional duct cleaning every 3-5 years, or sooner if you have pets, allergies, recent construction, or notice musty smells or poor airflow.

Does duct cleaning really improve air quality?↓

Yes. Removing years of dust, pollen, pet dander, and mold from your ductwork can significantly improve indoor air quality and reduce allergy symptoms.

Do you also seal ducts?↓

Yes. We offer professional duct sealing using mastic and other proven methods to stop air leaks that waste energy and reduce comfort.

How long does duct cleaning take?↓

Most residential duct cleaning jobs are completed in 2-4 hours. We use powerful truck-mounted equipment for thorough results.

Will duct cleaning help my HVAC system last longer?↓

Yes. Clean ducts reduce strain on your blower motor and help your HVAC system run more efficiently, potentially extending its lifespan.
