COMMON QUESTIONS

Windows & Doors FAQs

How do new windows and doors help with energy bills in Georgia?↓

Modern double or triple-pane windows with low-E coatings and argon gas can reduce heating and cooling costs by 15-30% while improving comfort and reducing outside noise.

Do you install all types of windows and doors?↓

Yes. We install vinyl, fiberglass, and wood windows, plus entry doors, patio doors, storm doors, and specialty windows. We work with top brands like Andersen, Pella, Marvin, and more.

How long does window replacement take?↓

Most homes can have all windows replaced in 1-3 days. We handle removal of old windows, installation, and cleanup so your home stays comfortable throughout the process.

Do you offer financing for windows and doors?↓

Yes. We offer financing through Optimus (GreenSky, Wisetack) and special senior/military discounts to make energy-saving upgrades affordable.

Will new windows increase my home's value?↓

Yes. Energy-efficient windows and doors are one of the highest-ROI home improvements. They improve curb appeal, comfort, and resale value.

Absolutely. Your HVAC system is only as good as your home's insulation and air sealing. We regularly see 30-50% energy savings when combining HVAC upgrades with insulation and air sealing.
