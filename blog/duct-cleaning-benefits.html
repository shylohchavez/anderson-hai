The Real Benefits of Professional Duct Cleaning in Georgia

Dust, pollen, pet dander, and mold in your ducts can make allergies worse and reduce HVAC efficiency. Here's what professional cleaning actually does.

By Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation • 7 min read • May 2026

If you or your family suffer from allergies, asthma, or frequent respiratory issues, your ductwork could be part of the problem. In Georgia's humid climate, ducts can become a breeding ground for mold, bacteria, and dust mites.

At Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation (formerly John Anderson Service Company), we perform professional duct cleaning as part of our whole-home energy approach. Here's what you can expect.

What Professional Duct Cleaning Actually Removes

  • Years of accumulated dust, dirt, and debris
  • Pollen and other allergens that trigger seasonal suffering
  • Pet dander and hair that circulates through your home
  • Mold and mildew growth (common in Georgia's humidity)
  • Construction debris from home renovations
  • Insects and rodent droppings (yes, it happens)

The Benefits You Can Feel

Our customers consistently report:

  • Reduced allergy and asthma symptoms
  • Better sleep and overall comfort
  • Improved HVAC efficiency and lower energy bills
  • Less dust settling on furniture and surfaces
  • Better smelling indoor air

The Anderson Difference:

We don't just vacuum the vents. We use professional equipment to clean the entire duct system, including the main trunk lines. We also inspect for damage and recommend sealing or repairs when needed. Our in-house sheet metal shop means we can fix issues on the spot.

How Often Should You Have Your Ducts Cleaned?

We recommend every 3-5 years for most Georgia homes. If you have pets, smoke in the home, or have recently done major renovations, every 2-3 years is better. New homes should be cleaned after construction dust settles.

Combine with Other Services for Maximum Impact

Duct cleaning works best when paired with proper insulation, air sealing, and HVAC maintenance. This is the whole-home approach that has earned us 513+ Google reviews.
