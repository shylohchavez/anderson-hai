Our In-House Custom Sheet Metal Shop with Laser Cutters

Why having laser cutters and a full fabrication shop right here in Calhoun gives you faster, better, and more precise HVAC work than anywhere else in Northwest Georgia.

When most HVAC companies need custom ductwork, they order it from a supplier and wait days or weeks for delivery. At Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation, we just walk into our own shop and cut it ourselves — often while you're still at home.

Our in-house custom sheet metal shop with laser cutters at 519 Pine Street in Calhoun is one of the biggest differentiators we have. It's not something most homeowners think about when they call for AC repair or a new system, but it makes a massive difference in quality, speed, and cost.

Why Custom Sheet Metal Fabrication Matters

Every home is different. Older homes in Calhoun and surrounding areas often have ductwork that was installed decades ago with round pegs forced into square holes. New construction sometimes has designs that don't match the actual layout of the house.

Off-the-shelf ductwork rarely fits perfectly. When it doesn't, you get:

  • Air leaks that waste 20-30% of your conditioned air
  • Uneven heating and cooling throughout the house
  • Higher energy bills
  • More strain on your HVAC equipment
  • Noisy operation

Custom fabrication solves all of these problems by creating ductwork that is designed specifically for your home's layout, your equipment, and your performance goals.

The Advantage of Our In-House Laser Cutters

Precision

Laser cutters create clean, accurate cuts with perfect joints. No gaps, no leaks, no compromises.

Cost Savings

Quality Control

We control every step from design to installation. If something isn't perfect, we fix it immediately — at no extra charge.

Real Examples from Calhoun & NW Georgia Homes

Over the years, our shop has created custom solutions for hundreds of local homes:

  • Historic homes in downtown Calhoun with unusual floor plans and tight spaces
