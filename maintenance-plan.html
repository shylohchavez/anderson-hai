Maintenance Plan FAQs

What is included in the annual tune-up?

We inspect all components, clean coils and burners, check refrigerant levels, lubricate moving parts, test safety controls, and replace filters. You'll receive a detailed report.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel online or by phone with 30 days notice. We pro-rate any unused months.

Do you offer the plan for both AC and heating?

Yes. The plan covers your full HVAC system — air conditioner, furnace, heat pump, or dual-fuel system.

What if I need emergency service?

Premium members get same-day priority and one free emergency diagnostic per year. Basic members get priority scheduling and discounted rates.
