Custom Sheet Metal Shop FAQs

What makes your sheet metal shop different?↓

We have in-house laser cutters and a full custom sheet metal shop right at 519 Pine Street. Most companies order from a distributor and wait days or weeks. Our in-house sheet metal shop means your ductwork is custom-fabricated to fit your home perfectly — not generic off-the-shelf pieces. It's all part of a complete system designed for your home.

Can you make custom ductwork for my home?↓

Yes. Our laser cutters allow us to create precise, custom duct sections, transitions, and fittings that fit your home perfectly — no more forcing round pegs into square holes.

How fast can you fabricate custom parts?↓

Most custom ductwork and sheet metal components are completed the same day or next day. No waiting for shipments from out of town.

Do you only do HVAC work or other custom metal too?↓

We specialize in HVAC-related custom fabrication (ductwork, plenums, transitions, etc.) but can also handle other custom sheet metal needs for your home or business.

Can I visit your shop?↓

Absolutely. Our shop is at 519 Pine Street in Calhoun. Call ahead and we'll be happy to show you the laser cutters and custom fabrication process in action.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does HVAC repair cost in Calhoun GA?

Typical AC or furnace repair in Calhoun ranges from $150 to $450 depending on the issue. We always provide upfront pricing before any work begins — no surprises.

Do you offer same-day or emergency HVAC service?

Yes. We offer same-day service for emergency repairs in Calhoun, Dalton, Rome, and all of Northwest Georgia. Call (706) 629-0749 for immediate help.

Is insulation worth it if I already have a new HVAC system?

Absolutely. Your HVAC system is only as good as your home's insulation and air sealing. We regularly see 30-50% energy savings when combining HVAC upgrades with insulation and air sealing.

What financing options do you offer for HVAC and insulation?

We partner with Optimus (GreenSky and Wisetack) for flexible financing through our approved lending partners. Approval, terms, and monthly payments vary by customer, project, and credit profile. Ask about example payment scenarios when you call.

Are you BPI certified?

Yes. We are the only BPI Certified HVAC company in the area. This means we look at your whole home's energy performance, not just the equipment.
