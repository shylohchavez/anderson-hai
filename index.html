ABOUT ANDERSON HAI

Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation — Family-Owned Since 1978

Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation is a family-owned HVAC, insulation, and whole-home energy company based at 519 Pine Street in Calhoun, Georgia 30701. The company was founded as John Anderson Service Company in January 1978 and now serves homeowners across Northwest Georgia — including Calhoun, Dalton, Rome, Cartersville, Adairsville, Chatsworth, Jasper, Ellijay, Fairmount, and Resaca — under the Anderson Heating, Air & Insulation name. As of 2026, Anderson HAI has been family-owned for 48 years, holds BPI, NATE, and ACCA certifications, and maintains a 4.8-star rating from 632+ Google Reviews. The company can be reached at (706) 629-0749, Monday–Friday 7:30am–5:00pm, with 24/7 emergency service available. Services include HVAC repair, HVAC installation, heat pumps, mini splits, insulation, air sealing, duct cleaning, indoor air quality, crawlspace encapsulation, energy audits, water heaters, custom sheet metal fabrication, and Georgia Power / HER / HEAR rebate-eligible energy upgrades.
