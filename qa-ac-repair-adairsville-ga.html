How do I know if I need ac repair in my Adairsville home?

Common signs: vents blowing room-temperature air, longer-than-usual run cycles, a sudden bump in your power bill, ice on refrigerant lines, weird grinding or buzzing from the outdoor unit, or the system short-cycling. In Adairsville, where some homes mix propane heat with separate AC, we also tell folks to listen for outdoor units that start but immediately shut down — that's often a hard-start capacitor or contactor issue, easy to fix if caught early. A free in-home assessment lets us check airflow, refrigerant pressure, electrical, and ductwork together rather than guessing from symptoms alone.
