How do I know if I need ductless mini-splits in my Jasper home?

Ductless makes sense in Jasper when you've got a space the central system can't reach efficiently — a finished basement, a bonus room over a garage, an addition, a sunroom, a vaulted great room with a hot loft. It's also the right move when a 1990s cabin's existing ducted system is failing and the choice is rebuild the ductwork or skip ducts entirely. At elevation with sub-15°F winter cold snaps, cold-climate inverter heat pumps often beat baseboard or strip heat economics inside three to five years. We run the math on your specific home and tell you when it doesn't pencil — not every home needs ductless.
